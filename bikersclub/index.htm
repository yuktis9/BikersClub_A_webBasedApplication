A bicycle club is a group of individuals whose primary interest and activities involve bicycles.

Most clubs are either organized around a brand or make, or around a type of riding (e.g. touring) Bikers clubs vary a great deal in their objectives and organizations. Mainstream motorcycle clubs or associations typically have elected officers and directors, annual dues, and a regular publication. They may also sponsor sports events and annual or more frequent bikes rallies where members can socialize.
